Calculate the true cost of any trucking route before you hit the road.

Truck Route Calculator helps truck drivers and fleet operators estimate fuel costs, overnight stays, and total trip expenses with precision.

KEY FEATURES:

• Real-Time Route Calculation
Enter your origin and destination to get accurate distance and route information powered by Apple Maps.

• Smart Fuel Cost Estimation
Calculates fuel costs based on your truck's weight, load, and actual fuel efficiency. Heavier loads? The app automatically adjusts MPG using industry-standard formulas.

• Overnight Stay Planning
Automatically suggests overnight stops based on DOT Hours of Service regulations (550 miles/day at 50 mph average). Adjust manually if needed.

• Live Fuel Prices
Fetches current diesel prices from the U.S. Energy Information Administration for accurate estimates.

• Cost Breakdown
See exactly where your money goes: fuel costs, overnight stays, total cost, and cost per mile.

• Save & Share
Save routes for future reference. Share detailed cost quotes with dispatchers or clients.

• Route History
Access your saved routes anytime. Reload previous calculations with one tap.

PERFECT FOR:
- Owner-operators calculating job profitability
- Fleet managers estimating trip costs
- Dispatchers creating accurate quotes
- Trucking companies planning routes

All calculations stay on your device. No account required.
